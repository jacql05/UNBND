FAQ

Do you handle bookings?
No. We don't process bookings. We drive qualified traffic to your WhatsApp / WeChat / Telegram or calendar link. On request, we can set up auto‑replies and booking widgets that hand off to you.
When do results show?
After launch, first inquiries typically appear in 3‑10 days (platform approvals + budget dependent). Expect steadier performance after 2‑4 weeks of optimization.
What about paid ads?
Ad spend is separate. You pay platforms directly. We manage targeting, creatives, compliance, and optimization to your budget cap.
Languages?
We support EN / 中文 / 日本語 / 한국어 / Bahasa Indonesia with market‑specific tone and phrasing.
Is photography required?
Not required, but recommended for rebranding. Complete Package includes photographer + makeup & hairstylist. We can also work with your existing photos.
Can you remove bad reviews?
No. We follow platform policies. We help you report policy‑violating reviews, craft professional responses, and push down negatives with fresh content, SEO, and ads.
Is this compliant in Australia?
Yes. We use a checklist aligned to Australian advertising rules and screen content before ads go live.
How fast can I go live?
Most first campaigns go live in 3‑5 business days after onboarding.
Do you work with agencies?
Yes. We support independents and agencies with separate playbooks.
